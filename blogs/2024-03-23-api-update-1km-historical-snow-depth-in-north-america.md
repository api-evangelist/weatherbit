<h2 id="improved-historical-snow-depth-accuracy">Improved historical snow depth accuracy</h2><img alt="API Update: 1KM Historical Snow Depth in North America" src="https://blog.weatherbit.io/content/images/2024/03/sfav2_CONUS_6h_2024032218.png" /><p>The <a href="https://www.weatherbit.io/api/historical-weather-daily?ref=blog.weatherbit.io" rel="noreferrer">Daily Historical API</a> now sources 1 KM spatial resolution snow depth data from the <a href="https://nsidc.org/data/g02158/versions/1?ref=blog.weatherbit.io" rel="noreferrer">NSIDC SNODAS</a> analysis. This update greatly improves historical snow depth accuracy in North America, especially in high terrain regions. </p><h2 id="conclusion">Conclusion</h2><p>We hope that our users find value in these updates.
